Description

MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES
This is a home based, professional field sales opportunity, located in or near a major metropolitan area. The primary responsibilities of this position include selling orthodontic products and custom lab services to General Dentists, Pediatric Dentists, and other related non-orthodontic dental specialties, by building strong relationships in these segments. Additional responsibilities include growing and managing a defined sales territory to achieve revenue targets, providing technical support and service within the defined territory, conducting in-office demonstrations, and promoting and attending CE events and tradeshows. Expectations are to sell orthodontic products and lab services during field sales calls and by phone, to existing customers and new prospects with the intention of selling in new products, increasing utilization and upgrading existing business to premium products.

This role will involve sole territory coverage, account management, telesales, lead generation, team selling, and office visits to new and existing customers.

In addition, you will be involved with various projects that support your sales efforts, including
  • Developing target lists and maintaining a CRM database.
  • Retaining and growing existing business, and generating new business opportunities.
  • Providing technical support and first class customer service to new and existing customers.
  • Developing and implementing marketing strategies to capitalize on trends within the defined territory through email, direct mail, and phone campaigns.
  • Conducting training for dentists and their staff via webinar and in-office visits.
  • Attending and presenting at CE seminars, trade shows and various sales meetings.

Job Requirements

EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE
Qualified candidates are required to possess a BA or BS degree (Science, or Business related degrees preferred), 1-3 years’ previous sales or marketing experience or 1-2 years working from home with a proven record of success in a quota driven environment. In addition, the ability to promote, sell, educate and present a diverse and technical product line are essential factors for success.

SKILLS / ABILITIES
Candidate must be a driven self-starter with a solid work ethic, excellent verbal and written communication skills, quick to build rapport, able to establish long lasting customer relationships in person and over the phone, and willing to cover a large geographical territory. Strong planning, organization, presentation, problem solving, and time management skills are also required. Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel focus), Adobe Acrobat XI Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and experience with Microsoft Dynamics or other CRM program are preferred.

SPECIAL QUALIFICATIONS
Candidate must be able to work from home unsupervised, have reliable transportation or access to local transportation as needed to service key customers, have access to high speed internet and phone service. Candidate must also be open to travel to dental conferences, trade shows, regional sales meetings, training events and annual corporate meetings.
